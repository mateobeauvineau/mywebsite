Our Origin

The Problem Was Personal

We started asking a simple question: why do small-to-midsize property owners have no good options? Enterprise systems cost $50,000–$80,000 and require hardware overhauls. Single-building tools don't scale. And the mid-market — owners managing 5 to 150 buildings — was being completely ignored.

We started talking to property owners. Dental practice owners, retail portfolio managers, real estate developers. Every conversation confirmed the same thing: they're drowning in disconnected systems, paying too much for energy, and have no affordable path forward.

So we built one. Apeiron is a software-first, system-agnostic platform that gives mid-market owners the intelligence that was previously only available to Fortune 500 facilities teams.

"The gap is obvious once you see it. Enterprise solutions are overkill. Single-building tools don't scale. The mid-market has been completely abandoned — and that's exactly where we operate."

— The Apeiron Team
